# 🌿 My Mind — A Digital Library

Welcome to the **source code** of my personal digital garden, built with [Quartz](https://github.com/jackyzha0/quartz).  
This is where I keep my notes, reflections, small life insights, and technical explorations — all woven together in a living repository of my mind.

🖥 **Explore the live site here:**  
👉 [my mind](https://nicurrego.github.io/my_mind/)

---

## 🚀 What's inside?

- **Markdown notes** organized by tags and folders
- My thoughts on technology, personal growth, AI, philosophy, and slices of everyday life
- The Quartz engine that turns these notes into a navigable, beautiful digital library

If you’re curious about how Quartz works or want to start your own digital garden, feel free to fork this repository and plant your own ideas.
